About

Groom & Bride

The Groom

Abhisek, the youngest son in his family, is known for his calm and cool demeanor. Sharp and handsome, he is a talented coder who transitioned from his early days as a theatre artist to becoming a successful software engineer. He is an avid gamer and binge-watcher, always up for a new adventure in both virtual and cinematic worlds. His love for exploration extends beyond the screen, as he dreams of traveling the globe, sharing new experiences and adventures.

Abhisek

The Bride

Rajosee, the eldest daughter in her family, is a blend of independence and strong opinions. Her discerning nature makes her particularly selective, but it’s this attention to detail that shines through in everything she does. A devoutly pious individual, Rajosee finds joy in the simple pleasures of life, whether it’s exploring new recipes in the kitchen, indulging in her love for reading, or keeping her surroundings impeccably clean.

On their first day at their first jobs, Abhisek and Rajosee never imagined they'd go from strangers to friends, let alone soulmates.

Amidst laughter and spilled dal, it was Abhisek's kindness in helping Rajosee that turned a moment of embarrassment into the start of a deep and lasting friendship.
